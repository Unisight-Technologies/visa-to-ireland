Why choose Ireland ?

  • Ireland is a safe and friendly location for family life.
  • Ireland's air quality and food products are world renowned.
  • Ireland has a world-renowned education system for students of all ages
  • Ireland offers a fantastic base for retirement.
  • Irelands private healthcare system is relatively inexpensive and has temperature climate.
  • Ireland has multiethnic populace and sparsely populated living environment.

Dublin, Irelands capital is now ranked 46th most expensive city in the world. Ireland is a neutral country and the 12th most peaceful country out of 163 nations to live. (2020 Global Peach Index) Irish citizens are free to travel, live, work or be educated in any European Union state.
